Our Company Story

Fintegic Investment is a new venture from the visionary leaders of Agro Services and Pesterminators, who are the leading solution providers on their respective fields. For past, several years Agro Services and Pesterminators have brought lasting changes and new technology to the industry. Now we proudly venture into Food & Beverage, Tourism, and Investment business. The company is inspired to introduce latest products and international brands in Maldives as its sister companies.


In this context, we have the vision to bring high quality consumer range of products, resort amenities and international spa range products at affordable prices. As such context, we have tied up with leading manufacturers and suppliers internationally, achieve our goal of having a wide range of quality consumer goods under one roof.


Our product ranges are used in Housekeeping, Spas, F&B in Resort segment and consumer segment ranging from cafes, restaurants, and offices. We are committed to supply any requirement on timely basis.


Our specialized resort amenities are customizable and can give the luxury feeling for your guests during their stay. All our partners have a well-established infrastructure suitable for producing good quality products. All our products utilize only finest quality raw material that is sourced from genuine vendors of the industry. Apart from this, these products are examined on well-defined quality parameters. This assures that only good quality products are supplied.
