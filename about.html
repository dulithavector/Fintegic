Fintegic Invesments

Introduction

Fintegic Investment is a new venture from the visionary leaders of Agro Services and Pesterminators, who are the leading solution providers on their respective fields. For past several years Agro Services and Pesterminators have brought lasting changes and new technology to the industry. Now we proudly venture into Food & Beverage, Tourism and Investment business. The company is inspired to introduce latest technologies in Maldives as its sister companies.


In this context, we have the vision to bring good quality consumer range of products, at affordable prices. In this context, we plan to tie up with leading manufacturers internationally, achieve our ultimate goal of having a wide range of quality consumer goods under one roof.


This proposal is to introduce the Hyson range of tea leafs, Infusion tea and flavoured tea to our clients.


PARTNERSHIP

Fintegic Investments have partnered with several international suppliers to bring the latest and quality products to the Maldivian consumers. We have partnered with Empire Teas Pvt Ltd, to market the high quality tea varieties to Maldives.

There are a lot of tea brands currently in the market, but no brand has the Flavoured tea, Organic Tea, Infusion Tea, Specialty tea, Tea gift collection and special pyramid range as whole. Hyson teas has this unique basket, which prompted us to tie up with them, to bring in something new to the market.

Empire Teas (Pvt) Ltd is a leading exporter of quality Ceylon Teas which is among the top three exporters of Sri Lanka. With an annual export volume of over 14 million kg , it has a wide range of value added products to comply with every taste, culture, mood and moment under “Hyson”, “Regalo” and “Mervin” brands. Since its inception, Empire Teas has been successful in marketing products across the globe, particularly to the Western World, Far East, Middle East, Russia & CIS, USA and other countries in Eastern Europe. Empire is an ISO 22000:2005, ISO 9001:2000 , BRC, HACCP, Organic, Fair-Trade and Halal Products certified company. The team of professionals &

dynamic Tea Traders possess a collective experience of many years in international tea tasting, blending, grading and trading.

The company is fully focused in marketing their brands whilst maintaining its values, vision and image. Commitment is at the highest level in order to deliver the brand promise to all the present and potential consumers. Empire teas come in diverse forms of packets, tea bags, metal tins, gift items, canisters containing a variety of black and green teas ranging from regular, flavored, fruit, specialty blends, herbal and RTD (Ice teas). Teas are packaged in vibrantly colorful, innovative and attractive packs which are unique to Empire range of products.

Food Empire Holdings Limited, Singapore (www.foodempire.com) is a partner of Empire Teas Sri Lanka. Food Empire has 20 offices worldwide and sales in over 50 countries. It can boast of being an organization which has very few parallels in terms of market knowledge, consumer knowledge and wide spread reach. Added to this is Empire Tea’s expertise knowledge of Ceylon tea, which adds value to the business. At the same time Empire provide Specialty Teas and flavoured blends with natural fruits and flowers.
