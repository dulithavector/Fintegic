Hyson Tea

Fintegic Investments have partnered Empire Teas Pvt Ltd, to market the high quality tea varieties to Maldives.

There are a lot of tea brands currently in the market, but no brand has the Flavoured tea, Organic Tea, Infusion Tea, Specialty tea, Tea gift collection and special pyramid range as whole. Hyson teas has this unique basket, which prompted us to tie up with them, to bring in something new to the market.

Empire Teas (Pvt) Ltd is a leading exporter of quality Ceylon Teas which is among the top three exporters of Sri Lanka. With an annual export volume of over 14 million kg , it has a wide range of value added products to comply with every taste, culture, mood and moment under “Hyson”, “Regalo” and “Mervin” brands. Since its inception, Empire Teas has been successful in marketing products across the globe, particularly to the Western World, Far East, Middle East, Russia & CIS, USA and other countries in Eastern Europe. Empire is an ISO 22000:2005, ISO 9001:2000 , BRC, HACCP, Organic, Fair-Trade and Halal Products certified company. The team of professionals & dynamic Tea Traders possess a collective experience of many years in international tea tasting, blending, grading and trading.

The company is fully focused in marketing their brands whilst maintaining its values, vision and image. Commitment is at the highest level in order to deliver the brand promise to all the present and potential consumers. Empire teas come in diverse forms of packets, tea bags, metal tins, gift items, canisters containing a variety of black and green teas ranging from regular, flavored, fruit, specialty blends, herbal and RTD (Ice teas). Teas are packaged in vibrantly colorful, innovative and attractive packs which are unique to Empire range of products.

Food Empire Holdings Limited, Singapore (www.foodempire.com) is a partner of Empire Teas Sri Lanka. Food Empire has 20 offices worldwide and sales in over 50 countries. It can boast of being an organization which has very few parallels in terms of market knowledge, consumer knowledge and wide spread reach. Added to this is Empire Tea’s expertise knowledge of Ceylon tea, which adds value to the business. At the same time Empire provide Specialty Teas and flavoured blends with natural fruits and flowers.

Food Safety and Traceability

At Empire, the company strive to continuously improve the quality of the products through R & D processes carried out in-house. Whether it’s being a value added product or bulk, the company take in a great effort to make the products suit to the current market conditions and trends.

The company’s prime concern is to ensure that Empire products are made mostly using recyclable and environmental friendly material. Most of the products are designed in a way that the package can be reused as well as made from recyclable raw materials to be environmentally friendly. Some of the products in the range are designed in a way that it can be used as a presentable gift during special occasions or seasons. Due to the high quality of the products they can be used as gifts for any occasion.

  • Empires very own laboratory is geared to test the composition of the teas and to eliminate any inferior        quality products.
  • The blending machines are equipped with state of the art cleaning facilities to provide the best possible        product to the end consumer.
  • Empires in-house designers and product development team is fully geared to cater to its own brand as well       as private label product development needs.
  • ‘Regalo’ branded tea bags are packed in one of the latest tea bag machines which produces metal free tea        bags at a very high speed.
  • Teas are blended, flavoured and packed with the use of modern machinery in order to provide a high        quality of product whilst maintaining high level of hygiene.

    • Empires products are made mostly using recyclable and environmental friendly material. Empire teas affirmations towards food safety standards are apparent in the many certifications awarded to the Company.

    All of Empire’s processes follow these stringent food safety standards. The storage facility and the Factory Premises are thus governed by international food safety standards while the Factory also comprises in-house laboratory testing facilities. Henceforth all teas received and exported are subjected to routine tests to affirm adherence to such protocols.

    Empire is able to provide detailed traceability reports that can be traced back to the Tea Garden itself. This affords the Company a level of transparency that assures its clientele the highest level of purity and Tea that is produced under strict adherence to the Company’s food safety standards.
